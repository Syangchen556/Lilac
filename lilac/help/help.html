AI Chatbot Help

To start a conversation, simply type your query in the chat box and press Enter or click the "Send" button. The chatbot will respond with the relevant information.

If you'd like to reset the conversation, simply click the "Reset Chat" button at the bottom of the chat interface. This will clear all previous messages.

If you're facing any issues, please visit the "Contact Us" section and submit your feedback or issue. Our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

To change the settings, go to the "Settings" section in the main menu. You can modify preferences like language, notification preferences, and theme.
